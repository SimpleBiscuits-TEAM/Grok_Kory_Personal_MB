# Fleet Management Competitor Research

## Samsara ($27B+ market cap, industry leader)
**Core Features:**
- Real-time GPS tracking with geofencing
- Vehicle diagnostics via CAN bus gateway
- AI dash cams (front-facing, multicam 360°)
- Driver behavior scoring & coaching
- Fuel management: idling alerts, fuel waste reporting, emissions tracking
- ELD compliance (HOS, DVIR, IFTA)
- Routing & dispatch
- Maintenance: predictive, remote diagnostics, fault codes
- Driver app with mobile workflows
- OEM integrations ecosystem
- EV fleet planning
**Hardware:** Vehicle Gateway (cellular + GPS + CAN bus), Powered Asset Gateway, Environmental Monitor, AI Dash Cams
**Pricing:** Custom quotes, typically $30-50/vehicle/month

## Geotab (largest commercial telematics provider)
**Core Features:**
- GPS fleet tracking
- Engine/vehicle diagnostics
- Driver safety reports & scorecards
- Fuel management: idling, fuel cost, fill-ups
- Fleet optimization: rules, alerts, speeding, idling
- Maintenance scheduling
- ELD compliance
- Accident detection & notification
- Zone & time rules
- 17 languages
**Hardware:** GO device (OBD-II plug-in)
**Differentiator:** Most configurable/customizable, huge marketplace of add-ons

## Motive (formerly KeepTruckin, #1 on G2)
**Core Features:**
- AI-powered safety: dash cams, driver coaching
- ELD compliance (HOS, DVIR)
- GPS tracking
- IFTA fuel tax reporting
- Spend management
- Maintenance
- Driver app
- ML models for automated safety
**Differentiator:** Best user satisfaction scores, strong AI coaching

## EZLynk (diesel performance + fleet)
**Core Features:**
- Cloud-based vehicle diagnostics
- Remote calibration updates (OTA)
- ELD compliance
- Fleet management dashboard
- DTC read/clear
- Live data streaming
- Auto Agent hardware (OBD-II)
- Multi-driver support
- Chat with fleet manager
**Differentiator:** Performance tuning + fleet in one device, diesel-focused

## Common Features Across All Competitors:
1. Real-time GPS tracking + geofencing
2. Vehicle diagnostics (fault codes, engine data)
3. Driver behavior scoring (speeding, braking, idling, acceleration)
4. Fuel economy monitoring & reporting
5. ELD/HOS compliance
6. Maintenance scheduling (preventive + reactive)
7. Alerts & notifications (to management)
8. Driver app
9. Reporting & analytics dashboards
10. IFTA fuel tax reporting

## V-OP Fleet Advantages (what we already have or can build):
- **AI reasoning engine** — not just data display, actual diagnostic intelligence
- **Health analysis** — already functional, just needs fleet-wide aggregation
- **Calibration knowledge** — we understand what the ECU is doing, not just reading codes

- **VOP OBD memory storage** — offline data collection, sync later via WiFi/BT/USB

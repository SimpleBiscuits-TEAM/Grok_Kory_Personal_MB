# PPEI HPTuners RTD4 & TDN App — Customer Setup Guide

## What You Need
- HP Tuners RTD4 device (plugs into OBD-II port, under dash, driver's side)
- TDN App (free — Apple App Store or Google Play Store)
- Smartphone or tablet (iOS or Android with Bluetooth enabled)
- PPEI Invite Code (changes every week — contact PPEI for the current code)
- Your vehicle (ignition must be accessible, key turned to ON for reads and info logs)

## PART 1 — INSTALL THE TDN APP

### Android
1. Open browser on phone/tablet
2. Go to: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.hptuners.tdn
3. Click Install

### Apple iOS
1. Open browser on phone/tablet
2. Go to: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tune-delivery-network-tdn/id1590560864
3. Click Install

NOTE: TDN app is free. You'll need a free HP Tuners account (Part 3). If you already have one, log in during setup.

## PART 2 — INITIAL RTD4 DEVICE SETUP & BLUETOOTH PAIRING
IMPORTANT: Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone before opening the app. Be near your vehicle.

### iOS Setup
1. Tap START SETUP
2. Select GOT IT for Bluetooth permission
3. Tap NEXT (twice)
4. App prompts you to connect RTD4 to OBD-II port and turn vehicle ignition on — do it
5. Tap NEXT, wait for STAT light on RTD4 to turn solid green
6. When STAT light is solid green, hold down the button on the front of the RTD4 until the BT light starts flashing
7. Tap NEXT — app scans for devices
8. App detects RTD4 and pairs via Bluetooth — confirm pairing if prompted
9. After pairing, app syncs with server. If firmware updates needed, they download now. Keep phone connected to internet and near device. Tap Done/Finish when complete.
10. Agree to license agreement: Tap I Agree, then ACCEPT

NOTE: HP Tuners recommends re-syncing immediately after pairing. Tap TOOLS > Resync RTD4.

### Android Setup
1. Accept Website Privacy Policy — tap ACCEPT
2. Grant Bluetooth permission — tap OK (grant again if prompted)
3. App says it will configure TDN-compatible device — tap OK
4. Tap NEXT (twice)
5. App prompts to connect RTD4 to OBD-II port and turn ignition on — do it
6. Tap NEXT, wait for STAT light to turn solid green
7. When STAT light is solid, hold down button on RTD4 until BT light starts flashing
8. Tap NEXT — app scans for devices
9. App detects RTD4 and pairs — confirm if prompted
10. App syncs with server, may download firmware updates. Keep phone connected and near device. Tap Finish when done.

NOTE: If trouble connecting during setup, exit wizard, log in via ACCOUNT button, then retry. Can also re-run setup via TOOLS > Setup RTD4.

## PART 3 — CREATE YOUR HP TUNERS ACCOUNT
(Skip to Part 4 if you already have an account)
1. Tap ACCOUNT in navigation bar
2. Tap Create Account — browser opens to HP Tuners My Account page
3. Fill in required info and click Create
4. Return to TDN app, enter credentials
5. Tap LOGIN, enter credentials, tap Sign in

NOTE: Fill out your account profile (EDIT PROFILE > enter contact details > SAVE PROFILE) so PPEI has your contact info.

## PART 4 — LINK YOUR ACCOUNT TO PPEI TUNING
IMPORTANT: The PPEI Invite Code changes every week. Do not use an old code. Contact PPEI for the current code before starting.

1. Tap ACCOUNT in navigation bar
2. Tap LINK TUNER tab
3. Enter the PPEI Invite Code and tap SUBMIT
4. Confirmation screen appears — tap OK
5. PPEI auto-accepts almost immediately. "PPEI Tuning" appears under LINKED TUNERS
6. Tap JOIN to finalize

NOTE: You only need to join once. Even though the invite code changes weekly, your account stays linked after the initial connection.

## PART 5 — GATHER AN INFO LOG AND EMAIL TO PPEI
IMPORTANT: Turn vehicle key to ON position before gathering Info Log. Engine does not need to be running. RTD4 must be plugged into OBD-II port and connected via Bluetooth.

1. Turn vehicle key to ON position (engine off is fine)
2. Connect RTD4 to OBD-II port. Confirm STAT light is solid green and Bluetooth is connected in TDN app
3. Tap TOOLS icon on navigation bar
4. Select "Gather Info Log for Tech Support"
5. Tap GATHER INFO LOG
6. When finished, tap SHARE
7. Select "Share via Email / Messaging" and email to: support@teamppei.zohodesk.com

NOTE: When emailing, include your name, vehicle year/make/model, and a brief description of your modifications so PPEI can match the log to your order.

## PART 6 — READ YOUR VEHICLE
IMPORTANT — CAUTION: While the read is in process, do NOT start the engine, turn the ignition off, or unplug the interface device. Keep your phone close to the device and connected to the internet throughout.

Before you begin:
- Vehicle battery fully charged
- Phone battery fully charged
- Phone has good internet connection and Bluetooth on

1. Tap FLASH button in navigation bar — Tunes screen appears
2. Tap READ VEHICLE near bottom of screen
3. App prompts to connect RTD4 to OBD-II and turn ignition to ON (do NOT start engine) — do it
4. Tap OK to start the read. Takes several minutes. The app will:
   - Download/apply firmware or resource file updates to RTD4
   - Read tune data to RTD4's memory
   - Transfer data to your phone
   - Upload data to the server
5. When complete, a file folder for your vehicle appears at top of Tunes screen. Tap the vehicle name and verify a green check icon appears next to the read file.

NOTE: If green check is missing, the read succeeded but upload failed. Check internet connection, then tap SYNC FILES to resend. If still no green check, go to Account screen, log in again, retry sync.

## PART 7 — SYNC FILES TO PPEI TUNING
1. After vehicle read, tap the vehicle name in Tunes screen
2. Verify green check icon next to read file (means it's already uploaded)
3. If green check is missing, tap SYNC FILES — app connects to HP Tuners servers and uploads your read file
4. Wait for green check to confirm PPEI received your file
5. Done! PPEI will contact you when your custom tune is ready for delivery.

NOTE: Sync requires active internet (cellular data or Wi-Fi). When your tuner sends you a new tune file, it auto-appears in the list. If not, tap SYNC FILES to retrieve latest.

## WHAT HAPPENS NEXT
Once PPEI receives your read file and info log, we build your custom tune and push it back to your RTD4 via the TDN app automatically. When your tune is ready:
1. Tap FLASH button
2. Select your vehicle
3. Tap the tune file
4. Tap WRITE VEHICLE to flash your new tune
